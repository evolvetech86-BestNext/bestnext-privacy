Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · EvolveTech LLC

Data Controller

EvolveTech LLC operates the BestNext mobile application. For privacy questions, contact privacy@bestnext.app.

What We Collect

Account data: Your email address, collected via Supabase Authentication.

Profile data: Name, age, gender, height, weight, fitness goals, activity level, dietary preferences, lifestyle, and life focus areas.

Sensitive health data: Menstrual cycle information (cycle length, last period date) — collected only with your explicit consent and only when you select female gender.

Usage data: Completed missions, habit logs, XP, streaks, and pillar scores — stored locally on your device only.

AI conversations: Messages you send to the AI Coach, processed in real time to generate responses. Not retained after the session.

Why We Collect It

To authenticate your account and identify you across sessions. To personalise your AI coaching, daily missions, workout plans, and meal plans. To provide cycle-aware coaching (only if you opt in). We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

We process your data based on your explicit consent (given before any data collection begins) and the contract to provide the BestNext service. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

AI & Third Parties

Supabase: Provides authentication. Your email is stored in Supabase's infrastructure. See supabase.com/privacy.

Groq Inc.: Processes AI coaching messages in real time. Messages are not stored or used for model training. See groq.com/privacy.

Anthropic PBC: Fallback AI provider with the same data-handling guarantees.

Apple / Google: Handle payment transactions. We never receive your payment details.

No other third parties receive your personal data.

Sensitive Health Data

Menstrual cycle data is classified as special category health data under GDPR. It is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. It is only included in real-time AI coaching prompts sent to Groq/Anthropic, which do not retain this data. You can opt out at any time in Profile → Edit Profile.

Data Storage & Retention

Most data (missions, scores, habits, chat history) is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. Your email is stored in Supabase. Data is retained until you delete your account. Contact privacy@bestnext.app to request full deletion. Uninstalling the app clears all local data.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, erase, restrict processing of, and port your data. Email privacy@bestnext.app to exercise any right. We respond within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Children

BestNext is not directed at anyone under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Contact us immediately if you believe a child has an account.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes in-app at least 14 days before they take effect.
